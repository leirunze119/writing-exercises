我们从理论上分析了我们的机制的隐私属性，引入了集中的和概率的扩展差分隐私的新版本来解释所提供的保证。最后，我们将机制应用于基于本地模型中具有角距离度量的高维个人数据的朋友匹配。我们显示了现有的本地差分隐私机制在此应用中不起作用。我们还通过实验表明，我们的机制可以通过严格的隐私保护和高可用性使朋友匹配成为可能。

We theoretically analyze the privacy properties of our mechanism, introducing new versions of concentrated and probabilistic extended DP to explain the guarantees provided. Finally, we apply our mechanism to friend matching based on high-dimensional personal data with an angular distance metric in the local model. We show that existing local DP mechanisms do not work in this application. We also show through experiments that our mechanism makes possible friend matching with rigorous privacy guarantees and high utility.
