我们假设存在一个受信任且值得信任的管理者，将各个个体的数据保存在一个数据库中，通常由…行构成。直觉上来说，每一行都包含一个单个个体的数据，并且仍然直观地说，隐私目标是同时保护每个个体行，同时允许对整个数据库进行统计分析。

We assume the existence of a trusted and trustworthy curator who holds the data of individuals in a database, typically comprised of some number … of rows. The intuition is that each row contains the data of a single individual, and, still speaking intuitively, the privacy goal is to simultaneously protect every individual row while permitting statistical analysis of the database as a whole.
